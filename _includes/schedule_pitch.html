Information for pitch marshals

  1. Make sure the teams for the next match are ready to come to the pitch in plenty of time
  2. The referee may ask you to time the game. Check with him if there should be a half time or if they are playing straight through.
  3. Look after the match ball! Retrieve it from the pitch at the end of the game and hand it to the referee for the next game.
  4. If there are any safeguarding problems or other incidents, please make the Blackheath head coach for your age group aware immediately.
